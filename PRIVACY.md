# Privacy Policy for FlowBoard

Last updated: November 16, 2025

## Introduction

FlowBoard ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iPad task management application.

## Data Collection and Storage

### Local Data Storage
- **All task data is stored locally on your device** using SwiftData
- Task information (titles, descriptions, priorities, due dates, tags, and subtasks) is stored in an SQLite database on your iPad
- **We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers**
- **We do not have access to your task data**

### App Group Sharing
- Task data is shared between the main app, widget extension, and share extension using iOS App Groups
- This sharing happens exclusively on your device and does not involve any external servers
- The shared data remains under your control and is protected by your device's security measures

## Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:
- Personal identification information
- Usage analytics or statistics
- Crash reports (unless you explicitly opt-in through iOS system settings)
- Location data
- Device information
- Network activity data

## Third-Party Services

FlowBoard does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Notifications

- FlowBoard requests permission to send local notifications for task reminders
- These notifications are generated locally on your device
- No notification data is sent to external servers
- You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings

## Spotlight Integration

- FlowBoard indexes your tasks in iOS Spotlight for search functionality
- This indexing happens locally on your device
- You can manage Spotlight integration through iOS Settings

## iCloud and CloudKit

- **Currently, CloudKit synchronization is explicitly disabled**
- All data remains local to your device
- Future versions may offer optional iCloud sync, which would be clearly disclosed and require your explicit consent

## Data Security

- Your task data is protected by your device's built-in iOS security features
- We recommend enabling device passcode/biometric authentication
- App Groups are sandboxed and isolated from other apps on your device

## Data Retention and Deletion

- You have full control over your data
- You can delete individual tasks at any time within the app
- You can delete all tasks using the "Delete All Tasks" option in the app
- Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local data
- There is no data recovery as we do not maintain backups on external servers

## Children's Privacy

FlowBoard does not knowingly collect or store data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
- Providing an in-app notification (for significant changes)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access all your data (visible directly in the app)
- Modify your data (edit any task)
- Delete your data (delete individual or all tasks)
- Export your data (contact us for assistance)
